Culinary Arts Food and Beverage Services

Students will have a solid basis to establish a successful career in the food service sector thanks to the Culinary Arts and Food Services curriculum. The fundamental principles and real-world applications of the culinary arts and food services sector will be introduced to the students. Registration Requirements: Grade 12 or equivalent, Guardian & learner ID copies, completed registration document. Proof of residence & Two ID photos.
