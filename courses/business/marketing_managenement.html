What is a Marketing Management

Management is the process of carrying out tasks in an orderly and effective way. The goal of marketing management is to operate marketing activities effectively. The transaction of ownership of goods and services from seller to buyer is made easier by marketing management.

Career Opportunities

A career implies that you continue to do the same sort of work. A career might consist of one or many jobs. You can work for one or more firms. A "career path" is another term for a career. Education, training, and job experience all contribute to a career.

Addmission Requirements

This qualification seeks to equip students with the the knowledge and practical skills required to perform effectively as supervisors/junior level business managers or potential managers in establishments, control and effective management or private and public business ventures. It also empowers that individuals already involved in governing business on how to maintain cost effective and viable commercial entities

A National Senior Certificate with Diploma admission - A minimum of 30% for English coupled with a minimum of 40% for four (4) x 20 credit subjects; OR A NC(V) with Diploma admission – A minimum of 50% in English on either First Additional Language or Home Language level AND Mathematics or Mathematical Literacy AND Life Orientation – a minimum of 60% in the three (3) compulsory vocational subjects is required; OR A SC (without endorsement) or equivalent – A minimum of five (5) subjects must be passed; OR A Higher Certificate, an Advanced Certificate in a cognate field.

Mature Age Exemption:

  • Candidates must have attempted Matric/Grade 12, reached the age of 23 before or during the first year of registration with three years’ work experience and a proven ability relating to the proposed programme as well as adequate communicative skills;
  • Candidates must have reached the age of 45 before or during the first year of registration.

Start dates

Duration

Each level takes 6 Months full time to complete (total of 18 months to complete the theory) and then 18 months practical in industry. Generally it takes 3 years to qualify for a National Diploma.

Qualification

A registered certificate is awarded on successful completion of each programme.
