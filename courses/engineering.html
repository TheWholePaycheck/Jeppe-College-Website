Engineering N1 - N3

This qualification is designed to provide an opprtunity for learners to have an understanding of technical Engineering principles and skills. The course futher empowers students with analytical abilities which involves mathematical calculations and manipulation, hand -skills (practical skills) and practical capabilities. Admission Requirements: Fundamental competenices in the areas of Communication, mathematic literacy and life skills at a NQF Level 3. The ability to use a personal computer, training band up to NQF Level 2, competent and computing at NQF Level 1.
