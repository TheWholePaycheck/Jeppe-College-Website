Our Vision

" We have been running for 25 years, and we believe educational and training are for all. We are committed to equal opportunities for learners and staff, and we value the cultural diversity of South Africa. The college success will be built upon a sense of personal and professional achievement by everyone who studies and works here. We believe that raising educational achievement will contribute to the economic, social and cultural prosperity of South Africa. "

Courses Offered

Our curriculum is reviewed annually to keep pace with modern trends of providing education and training and enacting changes as required by the South African Qualification Authority (SAQA).

Engineering Studies

This qualification is designed to provide an opportunity for learners to have an understanding of technical Engineering principles and skills.

Information Technology

This qualification is designed to educate the learner how to utilize computers to fulfill regular business requirements and begin or enhance your career in the IT sector.

Business Studies

This qualification seeks to equip students with the knowledge and practical skills required to perform effectively as supervisors/junior level business managers or potential managers in establishments, control and effective management or private and public business ventures.

School of Culinary Arts

The fundamental principles and real-world applications of the culinary arts and food services sector will be introduced to the students.
