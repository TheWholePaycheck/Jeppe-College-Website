Courses Offered

Engineering Studies

This qualification is designed to provide an opportunity for learners to have an understanding of technical Engineering principles and skills.

Information Technology

This qualification is designed to educate the learner how to utilize computers to fulfill regular business requirements and begin or enhance your career in the IT sector.

Business Studies

This qualification seeks to equip students with the knowledge and practical skills required to perform effectively as supervisors/junior level business managers or potential managers in establishments, control and effective management or private and public business ventures.

The fundamental principles and real-world applications of the culinary arts and food services sector will be introduced to the students.
