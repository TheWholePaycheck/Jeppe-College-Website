About Jeppe College of Commerce and Computer Studies

About Jeppe College

Jeppe College of Commerce and Computer Studies is a Further Education and Training (FET) Institution that was founded to promote and uphold comprehensive education and training needs, with the goal of eliminating educational and training backlogs and imbalances.
Jeppe College is a nationally recognized FET Private Service provider, completely accredited by Umalusi, and licensed as a Private Education Service Provider by the Department of Education under the Private Further Education and Training Act, 2006 (Act 16 of 2006). We also provide globally recognized credentials.

Our Mission

To overcome educational inequities by offering a balanced learning environment supported by a carefully selected, highly educated team in order to develop well-rounded graduates.

Our Vision

To be a leader in providing the greatest quality education and training.

Our Values

We think that education and training should be available to everyone. We are committed to equal opportunities for learners and staff, and we value South Africa's cultural diversity. Everyone who studies and works at the institution will have a feeling of personal and professional accomplishment. We think that improving educational success will contribute to South Africa's economic, social, and cultural growth.

Background of Jeppe College

Jeppe College of Commerce and Computer Studies is a division of Jeppe Adult Education Center, which won the Guinness UDV Adult Literacy Award in 2001 for its unique and independent adult literacy provision in Johannesburg's inner city.
We are delighted that over 6000 learners have successfully finished courses in diverse sectors since the institution's founding, and many of them have either found employment or have received promotions at their current jobs. In 1997, the concept of constructing a learning center was conceived. The concept has since expanded into a big institution, with six locations in Johannesburg, Pretoria, Pietersburg, and Vereeniging. All centers are full-fledged educational institutions that provide a wide range of learning programs in both information technology systems and business studies.
Our main goal is to develop tomorrow's method of education by utilizing cutting-edge computer instruction, field work, and research. Jeppe College provides students with an exceptional opportunity to meet with future employers and top business entrepreneurs from South Africa and throughout the world. Students participate in a variety of research tasks throughout their courses, which appropriately prepares them for the job market.
Over the past years, we have set ourselves high professional standards, by interacting with leading institutions in the country, and by obliging our lecturers to keep track with changes in systems and methods of instruction.
Our curriculum is reviewed annually to keep pace with modern trends of providing education and training and enacting changes as required by the South African Qualification Authority (SAQA).

Learner Support

Print Email Learner Support Learner development is an important component of our quality management program, which aims to improve our learners' personal and professional growth. Personal development entails recognizing our learners' educational and training needs, as well as supporting and monitoring their personal and professional progress. It addresses components of personal growth such as social, economic, and emotional development, as well as self-esteem and confidence building. It goes far beyond simply learning new skills and information.
Our Learner Development Plan's overarching goal is to empower learners in this institution to critically examine and reflect on their living situations, with a willingness to explore new possibilities and create positive change.
Learners perform a learner development evaluation to determine the effect of the programs that are already accessible and in place. Learners must experience a sense of belonging to the college community. They are aware of their learning requirements and how to meet them. The entire college community, notably the students and their trainers, will feel good about themselves. The influence of the learners' development program will be made more clear to the learners.
